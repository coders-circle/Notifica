Notifica

A must have routine app

Always get notified, never miss a thing

This app not only keeps you informed about your upcoming classes, but also reminds you of exams and unfinished homework. Through the help of this app teachers can notify their students about what topics will be covered in the upcoming lectures and henceforth student can prepare on those topics prior to attending the lecture as a result of which student can get a complete understanding of what is being taught and the real time classes can be much more exciting and interactive.

Easily get and submit your Assignments

Notifica is designed to help teachers create and collect assignments paperlessly, including other time-saving features such as the ability to directly notify the class about the projects and assignments directly through the app without have to involve a third person like CR. It also incorporate interaction amongst the students about their project documentation and sharing ideas about the assignments and whereabouts of resources to find. Students can keep track of what's due on the Assignments area. Teachers can quickly see who has or hasn't completed the work, and provide direct, real-time feedback and suggestions and tutorials.

Time is important, save it

No time is wasted, you get instantly notified of what the next class and who is going to take the class, and if by any reasons the class is delayed or the class is off then you will be simply notified and you no more require to call your class representative for such information.
