Notifica is an app to ease the information flow inside an institution. It consists of web interface as well as smartphone app for the users. The app is currently available only on Android platform

If you are a student or a teacher looking for a good routine app with a beautiful and easy-to-use interface, Notifica is the perfect solution for you!

Always get notified, never miss a thing

Through the help of this app teachers can notify their students about what topics will be covered in the upcoming lectures.

Easily get and submit your Assignments

Notifica is designed to help teachers create and collect assignments paperlessly, including other time-saving features such as the ability to directly notify the class about the projects and assignments directly.

Time is important, save it

No time is wasted, you get instantly notified of what the next class and who is going to take the class, and if by any reasons the class is delayed or the class is off then you will be simply notified.
